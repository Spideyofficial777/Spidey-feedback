Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit feedback?

To submit feedback, simply navigate to the "Submit Feedback" section in your dashboard. You can choose the category, add a title and detailed description, and attach any relevant files. Once submitted, our team will review it and get back to you if needed.

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all feedback within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, we have a priority support system that guarantees a response within 4 hours for premium users.

Is my feedback anonymous?

Yes, by default all feedback is anonymous. However, you can choose to include your contact information if you'd like us to follow up with you personally regarding your suggestion or concern.

Can I track the status of my feedback?

Absolutely! You can track the status of all your feedback submissions in the "My Feedback" section of your dashboard. You'll see whether it's under review, implemented, or requires additional information.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to Account Settings > Privacy & Data > Delete Account. Please note that this action is irreversible and will remove all your data from our systems.
